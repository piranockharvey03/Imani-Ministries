What We Do

At Save the Future, we are dedicated to making a lasting impact through the following initiatives:

Education Programs

We provide access to quality education for underprivileged children, equipping them with the knowledge and skills to build a better future.

Healthcare Initiatives

Our healthcare programs focus on providing essential medical care, nutrition, and clean water to communities in need.

Baseball Outreach

We beleive that we not only minister to the world through the gospel alone but also engaging the young generation in a sport that would be of a great impact in their life

Advocacy and Awareness

We raise awareness about the Word of God
