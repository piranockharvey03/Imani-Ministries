What is Imani

School Sponsorship

We sponsor 26 children in our community to go to school. In Uganda, school is not free, making it inaccessible for most children in our village. Our goal is to get over 100 sponsored kids. You can help by becoming a monthly sponsor.

Baseball Ministry

Baseball has been the core of Imani since we first started 8 years ago. It provides a safe space for over 65 kids daily. Not only do they learn the sport, but they also receive mentorship, food, and teachings of the word of God.
